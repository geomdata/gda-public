multidim.models.GaussianMixtureClassifier.fit¶

GaussianMixtureClassifier.fit(*training)[source]¶

Fit this estimator to training data. This is the step that runs the actual predictor.

Parameters:
*training

Can be any of the following:

  • a pair of list-like objects: pointclouds_train and labels_train The items in pointclouds_train should be numpy.ndarray objects. The items in labels_train should be strings (preferably, color names).
  • a single labelled and weighted multidim.PointCloud
  • a single multidim.covertree.CoverTree obtained from a labelled and weighted multidim.PointCloud
